# English Hub — Product Context

## Product Purpose
English Hub is a Thai-language English learning platform for working adults who want to improve their English for career advancement. Learners study Grammar, Speaking, Listening, and Vocabulary through interactive exercises and AI-powered feedback. The platform tracks XP, streaks, and skill scores to motivate consistent daily practice.

## Register
product

## Users
- Primary: Thai working adults (25–40), studying English for job interviews, business communication, and promotions
- Secondary: University students preparing for TOEIC / IELTS
- Motivation: Progress visibility, gamification (XP, streaks, badges), short daily sessions (5–15 min)
- Context: Mobile and desktop, evening/weekend study sessions, often on the go

## Brand Tone
- Encouraging, clear, never condescending
- Thai-friendly: UI labels and instructions in Thai, exercise content in English
- Smart but approachable — like a patient native-speaking tutor

## Visual Identity
- Dark theme: deep navy background (#060f20), surface cards (#0c1830)
- Accent: sky blue (#38bdf8) primary, teal green (#22d3a0) secondary
- Typography: LINESeedSansTH for Thai, system-ui fallback
- No gradients on text; no side-stripe borders; no glassmorphism decoration

## Anti-references
- Not Duolingo's cartoonish green — this is professional, not playful
- Not a corporate LMS (gray tables, PDF downloads)
- Not a dark crypto/gaming aesthetic — blue is purposeful, not edgy

## Strategic Principles
- Skill scores are real: Grammar, Speaking, Listening, Vocabulary each 0–100
- XP is earned by completing exercises; scores update on each session result
- Level is derived from overall score: Beginner → Elementary → Intermediate → Upper-Intermediate → Advanced
- Features ship in order: Grammar → Vocabulary → Listening → Speaking
- AI Tutor (Ci mascot) is the differentiator — answers questions in Thai
